How to Use the Application

This application helps you manage your medication schedule efficiently by allowing you to add medicines, set dosage reminders, and keep track of your intake.

Step 1: Add Medicine

Click on "Add Medicine" to enter the medicine name, dosage, frequency, and duration.

Step 2: View Medicine List

Check the "Medicine List" to see all your added medications.

Step 3: Set Reminders

Go to "Reminders" to ensure you take your medication on time.

Step 4: Stay Organized

The system keeps your data safe, and you can edit or delete medicines anytime.
